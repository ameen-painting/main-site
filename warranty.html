The Details

What's Covered

Ameen Painting LLC provides a three-year warranty on all workmanship performed. This warranty covers peeling, bubbling, cracking, or splitting caused by improper surface preparation or improper application of coatings. If a covered issue arises, a member of our team will return to repair and touch up the affected area at no cost. This warranty is valid for three years from the completion date of the project and is transferable to a new owner or tenant.

Exclusions: This warranty does not cover damage caused by normal wear and tear, moisture intrusion, structural movement or settling, HVAC or humidity-related issues, accidental or third-party damage, vandalism, or paint failure caused by pre-existing substrate conditions such as rotted wood, prior coating failures, or surface defects not disclosed before work began.
