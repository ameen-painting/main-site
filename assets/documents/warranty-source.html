What's Covered

We stand behind our work and guarantee that all painting applications will be free from peeling, blistering, or chipping caused by defects in workmanship for a period of three (3) years from the date of completion.

Warranty Exclusions (What is NOT covered)

  • Damage caused by structural movement, foundation settling, or inherent drywall/plaster defects (including new or expanding cracks).
  • Damage resulting from moisture, water leaks, plumbing issues, roof leaks, or mold/mildew due to poor ventilation.
  • Mechanical damage, ordinary wear and tear, pet scratches, stains, or physical abuse.
  • Defects in the paint material itself (covered under the manufacturer's warranty).
  • Projects where the customer supplied their own paint or requested application over unstable/unprepared surfaces against our professional recommendation.
